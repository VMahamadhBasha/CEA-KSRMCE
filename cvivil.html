About the Association

Our Mission and Vision

Our Vision

To create a dynamic platform for KSRMCE Civil Engineering students to enhance their technical, managerial, and soft skills, making them industry-ready professionals and innovative leaders. We aim to be the premier technical association on campus, fostering growth in civil engineering domains.

Our Focus Areas

  • Organizing Workshops on advanced civil engineering software and techniques.
  • Conducting Technical Fests, Project Expos, and Design Competitions.
  • Mentorship programs with faculty, senior students, and industry experts.
  • Promoting research, sustainable practices, and innovative project development.
